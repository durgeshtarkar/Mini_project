Medical Conditions

Diseases We Detect

Our AI system can accurately identify and classify four major eye conditions that affect millions worldwide, enabling early intervention and treatment.

Normal Eye

Healthy retina with clear vision, normal eye pressure, and no signs of disease. Regular monitoring helps maintain optimal eye health.

Detection Rate 95%

Cataract

Clouding of the eye's natural lens, causing blurred vision and light sensitivity. Most common cause of blindness, but treatable with surgery.

Global Cases 95M+

Glaucoma

Progressive optic nerve damage often caused by high eye pressure. Known as the "silent thief of sight" due to gradual vision loss.

Risk Factor High

Diabetic Retinopathy

Diabetes-related blood vessel damage in the retina. Leading cause of blindness in working-age adults, but preventable with early detection.

Diabetic Risk 30%
